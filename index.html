Welcome To My Website!

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at University of Montreal and a member of CIREQ. I conduct research in macroeconomics, focusing on Public Finance and International Trade.

I study how governments can use fiscal rules to manage Public Debt and achieve sustainable Economic Growth. Additionally, I study how trade transmits shocks across countries and their macroeconomic implications.

Before my PhD, I obtained an MSc in Economics and Statistics, which equipped me with excellent quantitative skills for working with large data sets.

Research interests

  • Secondary field: Applied Econometrics, Climate Change
